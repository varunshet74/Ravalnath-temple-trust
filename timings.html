Temple Timings

General Temple Opening Hours

The temple complex is open from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day.

Darshan (Daily) Timings

Temple Morning Evening
Shivnath 7:00 AM – 1:00 PM 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Ravalnath 7:00 AM – 1:00 PM 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Mahamaya (Bhat Timings) 8:00 AM – 12:30 PM 3:30 PM – 7:00 PM

Abhishek Schedule

  • Shivnath:
    • Every Monday: 7:30 AM – 11:30 AM
    • Amavasya: 7:30 AM – 11:30 AM
    • Other Days: 7:30 AM – 8:30 AM
  • Ravalnath:
    • Every day: 9:00 AM – 11:30 AM

Palkhi & Weekly Activities

  • Shivnath: Every Monday Palkhi at 8:00 PM, followed by Aarti.
  • Mahamaya: Every Friday Palkhi at 8:30 PM, followed by Jogva and Aarti.
